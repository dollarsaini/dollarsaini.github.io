About us


Shiddat restraunt


Shiddat Thunder Bay specializes in authentic Indian Cuisine. Our food is freshly cooked and infused with the spices and culture of real Indian food. Our team and head chef have a combined 50 years in the food industry and specialize in the area of Indian food Dine-in is now available 7 days per week from 4:00pm – 9:00pm. We also do take-out and catering.
